Who We Are

Lento Group is a leading innovative conglomerate operating a number of businesses involved in different sectors of the economy. We deal with property investment and real estate development, construction and civil engineering projects, as well as manufacturing and production.

Our range of service includes:
• Property Investment & Estate Development
• Construction & Civil Engineering
• Manufacturing & Production of Aluminium Roofing Sheets
• Engineering Consultancy Services
• Machinery Supplies & Installation

We stay at the cutting edge of all our businesses and are dedicated to not only meet the needs of our clients but continuously exceed them. We have garnered a reputation for our efficient delivery and maintaining a highly professional approach in our operations. Our ultimate goal is the satisfaction of providing solutions to significant challenges faced by our clients through the value we bring.

As one of the top companies in the production, manufacturing, real estate and engineering services industries in Nigeria with a rapidly growing market share, we leverage on our highly regarded brand and more than 30 years of experience in our area expertise to provide our clients with the best that can be offered.
