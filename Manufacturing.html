Manufacturing & Production of Aluminium Roofing Sheets

“Roofing today, for tomorrow”

Lento offers a range of durable long-span aluminium roofing sheets and stone chip coated steel roof tiles. We are a prime manufacturer of corrugated aluminium roofing sheets with our method of production utilizing a continuous roll-forming process and our sheets are made to measure to meet customer’s requirements. Our colour coated aluminium sheets are specially treated in a coil–to–coil line, with thermoset acrylics/polyester coating, to reduce the glare of the sun, and to protect the sheets from atmospheric pollution and chemical attacks. The core strength of our stone coated steel roof tiles is Galvalume® steel (Al-Zn Alloy), which has both Aluminium-unique long-term corrosion and heat resistance and Zinc-unique “Galvanic Behaviour”.

The application of our products to the building/construction industry includes roof sheet, roof cladding, wall cladding, gutters, flashings, ridge capping, eaves flashing and installation that fits specification.

Over the years, Lento has been in the forefront of roofing across Nigeria, with an excellent record of successful completion of projects. Our client base cut across government ministries and agencies, non-governmental institutions, private organizations and individuals.
