Engineering Services

“Building the Future”

Our technical operations span the areas of Civil, Mechanical and Electrical Engineering. We have garnered core competencies in the construction and provision of engineering infrastructures such as roads, drainage systems, water reticulation, bridges, street lighting etc.

With our array of professionals ranging from Geologists, Architects, Quantity Surveyors, to Structural, Civil and Electrical Engineers, we can provide services that characteristically relate to planning, designing and managing of engineering infrastructure projects, designing and developing machines, materials, instruments and systems used in industrial processes, and also carrying out geophysical and other types of surveying and mapping.

We have worked with several clients across the industrial sector, the energy sector and the public sector to deliver completely new constructions, repairs, and maintenance of critical and support infrastructures. We are committed to the pursuit of Total Quality Management (TQM) in all our various projects and strive to achieve a balance between cost durability, effectiveness and time factors, which has earned us a reputation for delivering quality projects within time.
